Ingredients:
 - Rice 
 - Blended mixture of pepper, Tomatoes and onions
 - Diced liver
 - Chopped beef
 - Vegetable oil
 - Salt
 - Seasoning

Directions:
 1. Fry the blended mixture in some vegetable oil.
 2. Add salt and seasoning.
 3. Add the diced liver, the chopped beef and rice.
 4. Add enough water to cook the rice.
